Store Title & Subtitle Optimization

Title Format Rules

The Quest Store displays up to 30 characters in the title before truncation on most surfaces (browse grids, search results). The subtitle gets an additional ~80 characters and appears below the title on the detail page. Both are indexed for search.

Before (Weak)

Title: "Eggscape"
Subtitle: "A fun VR game for everyone"

After (Optimized)

Title: "Eggscape: VR Adventure for Kids"
Subtitle: "Free multiplayer eggscape rooms — solve puzzles, race friends, unlock worlds"

Why This Works

  • Title includes brand name ("Eggscape") + two primary keywords ("VR" and "kids")
  • "Adventure" evokes excitement for the 8-13 age group and matches search queries
  • Subtitle front-loads "Free" — the single most powerful conversion word for the kids/parent audience
  • "Multiplayer eggscape rooms" captures the exact long-tail query parents and kids search
  • Action verbs ("solve, race, unlock") create urgency and communicate gameplay variety

Alternative Titles to A/B Test

  • Eggscape: Free VR Eggscape Room — maximizes keyword density, may feel less exciting
  • Eggscape: Kids VR Puzzle World — leans into puzzle angle, good if puzzle content is primary
  • Eggscape: Multiplayer VR Quest — emphasizes social play, strong for friend-invite discovery
