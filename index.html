Gloomy Eyes & The Last Standing

An Immersive Location-Based Entertainment Experience

Where zombies and humans unite to restore the sun

Overview

Experience Type

Location-Based Entertainment (LBE) - An immersive theatrical experience where the audience becomes part of the story as zombie characters called "The Last Standing."

Core Narrative

A journey from underground imprisonment to freedom, as zombie survivors and humans work together to restore the sun and nature's balance in the mystical Alishan region.

Audience Role

Participants embody "The Last Standing" - zombie characters who must escape imprisonment, infiltrate enemy territory, and ultimately channel energy to restore the sun.

Central Theme

Unity between unlikely allies - zombies and humans - demonstrating that collective action can overcome tyranny and restore natural harmony.

The Story

Long ago, the sun retreated underground, causing the land to decay and zombies to emerge from the darkness. Under the tyrannical rule of Uncle, zombies are imprisoned and hunted.

The audience, as "The Last Standing," embarks on an epic journey guided by Gloomy - a child-like zombie with a pure heart - and Nina, a brave human ally. Together, they must escape captivity, traverse the mystical Alishan forest, infiltrate a guarded train, and ultimately reach the heart of the mountain where the sun's energy lies dormant.

Through sacrifice, unity, and the power of collective action, they discover that human and zombie hands raised together can channel the firefly light needed to awaken the sun and restore balance to the world.

Characters

Gloomy

Protagonist - Child Zombie

A young zombie with innocent eyes and a pure heart. Despite his undead nature, Gloomy represents hope and the possibility of redemption. He guides The Last Standing through their journey and ultimately makes the ultimate sacrifice to restore the sun.

Innocent Brave Selfless

Nina

Human Ally

A courageous human who defies societal prejudice to help the zombies. Nina frees The Last Standing from imprisonment and becomes a crucial bridge between the human and zombie worlds. Her capture later becomes a pivotal plot point.

Compassionate Courageous Defiant

Uncle

Antagonist - Tyrant

The tyrannical ruler who maintains power through fear and oppression. Uncle hunts and imprisons zombies, seeking to prevent the sun's return. His collapse of the cavern during the climax represents his desperate final attempt to stop the restoration.

Ruthless Fearful Powerful

The Gravekeeper

Narrator - Exposition

The mysterious storyteller who opens the experience by revealing the history of the sun's retreat underground. The Gravekeeper sets the stage and provides essential context for the audience's journey.

Wise Ancient Knowing

Pacha

Sage - Revelator

A wise figure who reveals the crucial secret: that collective human-zombie action can awaken the sun's underground energy. Pacha's revelation provides the key to the story's resolution.

Mystical Insightful Hopeful

The Last Standing

The Audience

YOU - the participants who embody zombie survivors throughout the experience. The Last Standing must escape imprisonment, fight alongside allies, and ultimately raise their hands to channel the firefly light that restores the sun.

Resilient United Transformative

The Eight Sequences

The narrative unfolds through eight distinct sequences, each building upon the last to create a complete journey from darkness to light.

1

The Opening

The Gravekeeper's Tale

The experience begins as the Gravekeeper emerges from the shadows to tell the ancient story. Long ago, the sun retreated underground, causing the land to decay. From this darkness, zombies emerged - not as monsters, but as victims of a world thrown out of balance.

Setting: Dark, atmospheric opening space Mood: Mysterious, foreboding
2

The Prison

Awakening in Captivity

The audience awakens as imprisoned zombies, held captive by Uncle's forces. The cells are cold and dark, and hope seems lost. But a glimmer of light appears as Nina arrives, risking everything to free The Last Standing from their chains.

Setting: Underground prison cells Key Moment: Nina's liberation
3

The Escape

Elevator & Arrow Combat

With Nina's help, The Last Standing breaks free. But escape is not easy - they must fight their way through Uncle's guards using arrow combat in a thrilling elevator escape sequence.

Setting: Industrial elevator shaft Interaction: Arrow combat gameplay
4

The Forest Journey

Rafting Through Alishan

Having escaped, The Last Standing meets Gloomy - a child-like zombie who will guide them on their journey. Together, they board a raft and travel through the mystical Alishan forest, navigating its misty waters and ancient trees.

Setting: Alishan forest waterways Interaction: Raft navigation
5

The Infiltration

Disguised as Rabbit Soldiers

Nina has been captured! To rescue her, The Last Standing must infiltrate a guarded train. Disguised as rabbit soldiers - Uncle's own troops - the group sneaks aboard, moving through carriages while avoiding detection.

Setting: Historic Alishan railway train Mood: Tense, covert
6

The Revelation

Pacha's Secret

Deep within the mountain, the group encounters Pacha, who reveals the secret to awakening the sun: collective human-zombie action. Neither group alone can restore the light - only together can they channel the earth's dormant energy.

Setting: Mountain cavern Key Moment: Learning the ritual
7

The Climax

Sacrifice & Resurrection

Uncle collapses the cavern. Gloomy sacrifices himself to protect the others. From the rubble, he resurfaces - transformed and empowered. Through synchronized hand gestures, The Last Standing channels firefly light to revive him.

Mood: Devastating, then triumphant Interaction: Hand-raising ritual
8

The Resolution

Dawn Breaks

The sun rises for the first time in ages. Dawn breaks over the Alishan mountains, and nature begins to awaken. Flowers bloom, the fog lifts, and balance returns to the world.

Setting: Mountain peak at sunrise Interaction: Flower touch effects

Key Scenes & Strong Moments

The most impactful moments that define the emotional journey of the experience.

Scene 1

The Gravekeeper's Prologue

Exposition

The experience opens in darkness. Slowly, a figure emerges - the Gravekeeper, ancient and knowing. Through whispered narration, the history unfolds: how the sun chose to hide, how zombies came to be victims, not villains.

Impact: Sets a tone of melancholy and mystery, reframing zombies as sympathetic figures.
Scene 2

Nina's Act of Defiance

Liberation

In the cold prison cells, all seems hopeless. Then Nina appears - a human who fears no zombies. With keys in hand, she unlocks the cells. This moment marks the beginning of the human-zombie alliance.

Impact: Hope ignites. The audience experiences their first taste of freedom.
Scene 3

Meeting Gloomy

Character Introduction

After escaping, The Last Standing encounters a small figure in the shadows. Gloomy steps forward - a child zombie with wide, innocent eyes. His gentle nature immediately endears him to the group.

Impact: Tenderness arises. Gloomy becomes the heart of the journey.
Scene 4

The Elevator Battle

Action Sequence

Alarms blare. Guards swarm. In a desperate escape through the elevator shaft, The Last Standing must fight back using arrow combat. The group battles upward toward freedom.

Impact: Adrenaline and agency. Participants become active warriors.
Scene 5

Drifting Through Alishan

Atmospheric Journey

The raft glides silently through misty waters. Ancient cypress trees tower overhead. Fireflies dance in the darkness. For a moment, the danger fades, and beauty takes over.

Impact: Wonder and peace. A meditative showcase of natural beauty.
Scene 6

The Rabbit Disguise

Infiltration

To board the train, The Last Standing must become what they fight against - Uncle's rabbit soldiers. Donning the enemy's uniform blurs the line between ally and enemy.

Impact: Tension and moral complexity about identity.
Scene 7

Nina's Capture Revealed

Plot Twist

Nina, who risked everything to free the zombies, has been captured by Uncle's forces. The rescue mission transforms from escape to salvation - paying back the debt of freedom.

Impact: Urgency and loyalty. Stakes become personal.
Scene 8

Pacha's Revelation

Turning Point

In the mountain's heart, Pacha shares the ancient secret: neither human nor zombie alone can restore the sun. Only together - hands raised in unity - can they channel the earth's dormant energy.

Impact: The theme crystallizes: unity is necessity.
Scene 9

Uncle's Desperate Strike

Catastrophe

As the group prepares the ritual, Uncle makes his final move. The cavern shakes. Rocks fall. In his desperation to prevent the sun's return, the tyrant brings the mountain down upon everyone.

Impact: Terror and chaos. The villain's true fear revealed.
Scene 10

Gloomy's Sacrifice

Climactic Sacrifice

As the cavern collapses, Gloomy acts without hesitation. The child zombie throws himself into danger to protect The Last Standing. His small form disappears beneath the rubble.

Impact: Devastation. The most innocent gives everything.
Scene 11

The Resurrection Ritual

Triumphant Climax

From the rubble, a glow emerges. Gloomy resurfaces, transformed. The Last Standing raises their hands together. Firefly light streams from their palms, channeling into Gloomy, who becomes the conduit for the sun's return.

Impact: Catharsis. The audience becomes heroes who bring back the light.
Scene 12

First Dawn

Resolution

The sun crests the mountain. Golden light spills across the Alishan peaks for the first time in ages. Flowers bloom at the audience's touch, releasing particles of light. Balance returns.

Impact: Peace and accomplishment. Collective action creates miracles.

Interactive Elements

The experience transforms passive viewing into active participation.

Arrow Combat

During the elevator escape, participants engage in arrow-based combat against Uncle's guards. This mechanic provides agency and tension, transforming the escape into participatory action.

When: Sequence 3 - The Escape Type: Combat/Defense

Raft Navigation

As the group travels through the Alishan forest waterways, participants control the raft's direction. This creates a sense of journey and exploration while showcasing the beautiful environment.

When: Sequence 4 - The Forest Journey Type: Navigation/Exploration

Movement Mimicry

Participants learn and perform choreographed zombie-to-human movements. This physical engagement reinforces the theme of transformation and unity between the two groups.

When: Throughout the experience Type: Physical/Choreographic

Flower Touch Effects

In the resolution sequence, touching flowers triggers beautiful particle effects. This gentle interaction symbolizes the return of nature's vitality and rewards exploration.

When: Sequence 8 - The Resolution Type: Environmental/Aesthetic

Hand-Raising Energy Ritual

The climactic interactive moment: participants raise their hands together to channel firefly light energy. This synchronized gesture directly causes the sun's restoration, making the audience the literal source of salvation.

When: Sequence 7 - The Climax Type: Ritual/Collective Action

Design Philosophy

Each interactive element serves the narrative. Combat creates stakes during escape. Navigation builds connection to the environment. Movement mimicry embodies transformation. The hand-raising ritual makes the audience essential to the story's resolution - they don't just watch salvation happen, they cause it.

Setting: Alishan, Taiwan

"Where ancient forests meet the clouds, and the railway carries stories through time."

The Forest

The Alishan forest is famous for its ancient cypress and cedar trees, some over a thousand years old. In the script, this mystical woodland becomes a sanctuary where nature's magic still lingers despite the sun's absence.

The Historic Railway

Alishan's narrow-gauge mountain railway is one of the world's most famous scenic routes. In the story, this train becomes a tool of oppression - Uncle's transport - and later a site of infiltration and rescue.

The Mountain Fog

Alishan is renowned for its sea of clouds and mysterious fog. This atmospheric element creates the perfect visual language for a world without sun - perpetually shrouded, beautiful yet melancholic.

Indigenous Heritage

The region's Tsou indigenous culture infuses the narrative with spiritual depth. The concept of humans and nature in harmony, the mountain as sacred space, and the sun as life-giving force all draw from these traditions.

Why Alishan?

The choice of Alishan as the setting is deliberate. This location represents Taiwan's natural beauty and cultural heritage - a place worth saving. The mountain's real-world association with sunrise viewing gives extra resonance to the story's climax: the return of the sun.

By grounding the fantasy in a real, beloved location, the experience creates emotional stakes that transcend the fictional narrative. Saving Alishan's sun means saving Taiwan's heart.
