Revenue waterfall Net revenues · 3 priority tiers
01

Off the top

HTC / Vive Originals recoups its 53.34% ownership share of the LBE Content IP revenues, per the Content Ownership Agreement. Any additional private financing partners secured after the Effective Date recoup at the same priority level, pro-rata to their contribution.

HTC 53.34%
Remaining 46.66%
02

Until 3DAR has received USD 300,000

From Atlas V's remaining share, until 3DAR has received total compensation of USD 300,000 (per the ALBYON ↔ 3DAR Production Services Agreement):

3DAR 80%
Atlas V 20%

If additional private financing is secured during production, 50% of that financing is paid to 3DAR and counts toward the USD 300k threshold.

03

After the USD 300,000 threshold

Once 3DAR has received total compensation of USD 300,000, all remaining net revenues are allocated equally:

3DAR 50%
Atlas V 50%

Atlas V provides 3DAR with semester revenue statements (within 30 days of each semester end) and read-only sales-dashboard access from day 1 of each venue. 3DAR may audit accounts once per year; if underpayment exceeds 5%, Atlas V bears audit costs.

Integration of Taiwanese cultural elements is required for milestone validation by HTC and the Taiwanese Ministry of Culture. These requirements prevail over any conflicting creative decision and are binding on both parties.

Other key terms
Multi-Location Same waterfall applies to all future venues. Atlas V notifies 3DAR ≥ 30 days before any new venue agreement.
New venue costs Hardware, space, install, setup = operator opex. Not "additional financing" — does not get off-the-top recoupment.
Showreel rights 3DAR may use stills + up to 3 minutes of excerpts on its website / showreels for non-commercial promotion.
Governing law Spanish law · WIPO mediation in Madrid · WIPO expedited arbitration in Geneva (English).
Confidentiality Survives termination by 3 years. Standard exceptions apply.
No assignment No party may assign, transfer, or sub-license rights without the other's prior written consent.
