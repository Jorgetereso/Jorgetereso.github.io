Official Imagery
The Premise

A World Without Sun

Long ago, the sun retreated into the mountains. Without its light, the land rotted — forests weakened, animals vanished, and the delicate balance between humans and nature collapsed. From that darkness, zombies emerged: not monsters, but consequences.

Under the iron rule of The Uncle, fear was weaponised into hatred. Survivors were imprisoned underground. The last of the zombies — you, The Last Standing — await execution.

One chance remains. A quiet young zombie named Gloomy is waiting above. And somewhere beneath the mountain, the sun still breathes.

The Cast

Characters

☠

Gloomy

Protector & Guide

A quiet, strange, brave zombie — essentially child-like. Carries simultaneous sadness and curiosity. He survived years alone, learning to listen to an erased natural world. His connection with Nina is simple and instinctive, like two children. Driven by courage, sacrifice, and a deep desire to restore balance.

ZombieProtectorSilent
✦

Nina

The Rebel Human

Guided by innocence and intuition. A strong heart, a rebellious spirit, and an empathy rare in the Uncle's world. She respects zombies when no one else does. She frees prisoners, defies authority, and becomes the key force in the world's rebirth. Brave, impulsive, deeply human.

HumanRebelliousEmpathetic
★

The Uncle

The Authoritarian Ruler

Egocentric, narcissistic, obsessed with control, purity, and appearances. He sees himself as the centre of the world — comparing his power to the sun's. Possessive of Nina, disguising domination as protection. Beneath his grandiosity lies deep insecurity and paranoia.

VillainAuthoritarianParanoid
▦

The Gravekeeper

The Philosopher

Strange, mysterious caretaker of forgotten places. Part poet, part philosopher, quietly bohemian. He observes the world with distance and reflection. He speaks with a calm ritual tone — enigmatic yet guiding at key moments, offering knowledge and warnings without stepping into conflict.

EnigmaticPoeticGuide
❀

Pacha

Ancient Forest Woman

Ancient, wise, deeply connected to nature's hidden systems. Her body is intertwined with roots, plants, and colourful fungi. She moves with extreme slowness and frequently falls asleep mid-sentence. Her presence embodies ancestral knowledge and living memory.

AncientNatureAlishan
♾

The Last Standing

The Audience — You

The last surviving zombies. You experience this world as newcomers — learning through movement, instinct, and collective action. You are not spectators. You are essential parts of the world's rebirth. Without you, the sun stays buried.

ZombiePlayerEssential

The Wild Boar

Gloomy's Companion

Young, small, agile, and fearless. Gloomy's loyal companion who moves quickly through the forest. He brings warmth, humour, and life into Gloomy's isolated world — even letting Gloomy use him as a mount during disguise training.

Ordinary humans wearing rabbit-like uniforms as a sign of devotion to the Uncle's cult. Completely submissive. Initially ghostly and intimidating, their appearance becomes subtly absurd — long pointed hoods resembling bunny ears.

The Script

Scene by Scene

INTRO

The Hiding of the Sun

⸻ Darkness. A distant warmth pulses from afar.

The Gravekeeper emerges slowly from shadow, telling the story of a world unmade. Narrated with small puppets that transform into living, self-moving characters. The sun retreated into the mountains. Without light, the land rotted. Zombies emerged as consequence, not as monsters. Fear bred hatred. Survivors were imprisoned. One chance remains: find Gloomy.

Prologue
01
EXECUTION ORDER

The Dungeons

⸻ Underground cages. Humans in rabbit costumes guard zombie prisoners.

The Uncle enters enraged. He demands faster executions — three minutes, with pain. Nina arrives; a tense confrontation reveals his possessive obsession. Massive unseen creatures respond to his command with explosive barking and glowing eyes. A single clap silences them. Then Nina strikes a guard, seizes a key, and forces open the door.

"Someone is waiting for you above." — Nina
▶ Players can grab arrows, pull them out, and throw them at others
02

First Escape: Gloomy

⸻ Surface. Bunny soldiers closing in.

Gloomy drops from above — silent, swift, precise. He circles the group, forcing soldiers back without a word. A magenta burst of light. He returns holding a soldier's white hood; the body left behind. Gloomy leads through tunnels to a small river where a wooden raft waits. The current carries them into the Alishan forest — tall trees, light through canopy, the first suggestion of restored life.

▶ Players help shift the raft's position to guide it correctly
03

Gloomy's Home: Bad News

⸻ An improvised dwelling — stacked platforms, planks, and ramps.

The wild boar appears, playfully engaging Gloomy. Walls covered in drawings — many featuring Nina. The Gravekeeper returns: Nina has been captured and locked away. They must board the Uncle's train. A training sequence begins — the group must learn to walk and behave like humans. Gloomy perches atop the wild boar, wobbling comically. A train rumbles in the distance.

▶ Players must copy the Gravekeeper's choreography to pass as human
04
HIGH TENSION

The Train: Undercover

⸻ A moving train. Narrow, tense interior spaces.

The group follows Gloomy through carriage after carriage of bunny soldiers. Then the Uncle appears — inspecting a device humming with extracted sun energy. He suddenly sniffs the air. Zombie. He explodes, kicking a soldier from the moving train. The carriage goes silent. The only way forward: a narrow ledge along the exterior, wind roaring, the ground far below.

05

Nina: Magic Interrupted

⸻ The final wagon. Nina imprisoned and guarded.

Gloomy frees Nina. She throws him to the floor — then recognises him. She detaches the wagon. On a broken bridge, Gloomy reaches back toward Nina. The moment their hands touch, light spreads across the structure — the bridge rebuilds. Leaves lift, flowers open, petals fall. Then the darkness stirs. The Uncle's pets have found them. A portal opens.

"Those things are wild. They're starving. If we stay here, we're dead." — Nina
▶ Players can touch passing flowers to trigger petal releases
06

Pacha: What Still Lives

⸻ A cave full of life — grown, not built. Roots twist through walls and ceiling.

Pacha moves slowly, drifting between intensity and sleep. Her revelation: the sun is not gone — it moved underground, into the roots, alive within the forest itself. She teaches how to connect with its energy. Fireflies gather. Then the Uncle breaks in and drains the energy from Gloomy's eyes. Gloomy collapses. Nina opens the portal and pulls the group through at the last second.

"Alone, it cannot wake up. Humans and zombies. Together! That is the only way." — Pacha
▶ Players raise and lower hands like Pacha, activating light particles
07
COLLAPSE

The Mountains: Everything Falls Apart

⸻ A world covered in fog. Cold, heavy air. Distant sound.

"Gloomy is gone." Nina keeps moving. The world shrinks into miniature — portals open and close across forests, cliffs, valleys, ruins — then expands back to full scale. Glowing footprints lead forward. The path narrows to a cliff's edge. A massive cavern carved inside the mountain. Light breaks through stone. The Uncle storms in. The mountain begins to collapse.

"Walk!! You're already dead anyway. Keep moving!!" — The Uncle
08
AWAKENING

Final Confrontation: Awakening the Sun

⸻ The Sun Cavern. A mountain collapsing around you.

Gloomy falls through a portal — eyes burning red, hair lifting like fire. The fight is violent and chaotic. The Uncle strikes Nina to the ground. Something breaks in Gloomy. He charges — one final surge — and throws the Uncle into the abyss. Then collapses. Nina raises her hands. The group follows. Thousands of fireflies pour into Gloomy. Light spreads. Slowly, he awakens. The sun rises.

"Do it! What you learned. Now." — Nina
▶ Players raise and lower hands with Nina — their collective motion awakens the sun
OUTRO

A New Dawn

⸻ Post-conflict. Light spreading across the land.

The Gravekeeper speaks one final time. The sun has returned. Air feels warmer. Animals slowly emerge; birds begin singing as the light grows stronger. Night recedes. Morning arrives for the first time in a long, long while.

"This world is fragile. It always was. But today, it lives. For now." — The Gravekeeper

Participation

You Are Not a Spectator

The Last Standing is an audience-driven experience. Every action you take has consequence. The world responds to what you do — together.

▶

Movement Choreography

Mirror the Gravekeeper's movements precisely to pass as human through enemy territory. Get it wrong, and the disguise fails.

✨

Light Particle Control

Raise and lower your hands in sync with the forest's energy. Your gestures directly activate hidden light within the natural world.

→

Arrow Manipulation

Grab arrows mid-flight, pull them out, and throw them. Zombies feel no pain — use that to your advantage.

♨

Raft Navigation

Shift your weight to guide the raft through underground river passages toward the light of the Alishan forest.

⚘

Flower Triggers

Touch the flowers that drift past on the broken bridge. Each contact releases a cascade of petals — a moment of magic between Gloomy and Nina.

☀

The Final Awakening

The most important action in the experience: raise your hands together with Nina. Without the collective motion of every single person, the sun stays buried forever.

Rooted in Place

Taiwan Cultural Elements

Gloomy Eyes is deeply embedded in the landscapes, wildlife, infrastructure, and indigenous heritage of Taiwan — specifically the Alishan mountain region.

🌳

Alishan Forest

The primary landscape draws from Alishan — Taiwan's iconic high-altitude forest of giant cypress trees, layered canopy, mountain presence, and characteristic mountain fog.

🚂

Alishan Forest Railway

The iconic red mountain trains of the Alishan Forest Railway — a historic narrow-gauge line built during the Japanese colonial era — serve as the setting for Scene 4.

🦅

Formosan Wildlife

Gloomy's wild boar references the Formosan wild boar. The Uncle's pets are inspired by the Formosan leopard cat, one of Taiwan's rarest native predators.

🏭

Tsou Indigenous Heritage

Pacha's wardrobe is drawn from the Tsou people — indigenous to the Alishan region. Her cave is inspired by Alishan's Three Generation Tree.

🌎

Sound & Atmosphere

Bird sounds inspired by native Taiwanese species. Interior mountain spaces reflect Taiwan's geological formations. A soundscape grounded in a deeply specific, real place.

🎄

Fireflies

Fireflies appear as a recurring motif throughout the experience. Firefly watching is a beloved cultural tradition in Taiwan's mountain forests, particularly around Alishan each spring.
